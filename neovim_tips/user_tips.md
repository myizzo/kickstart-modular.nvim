# Title: Split buffer in half 
# Category: Editing
# Tags: buffer
---
Split buffer with <leader>sp

***
# Title: Fullscreen a buffer 
# Category: Editing
# Tags: buffer
---
Fullscreen buffer with <leader>ak
***
# Title: Toggle term 
# Category:  term
# Tags: terminal
---
Open toggle term with <leader>0
Leave it with <C-w>q
Move to it with <C-j> or <C-h> if opened (normal mode)
